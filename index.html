ABOUT ME

Hello world!

I served in the U.S. Army and California National Guard. My experience in the military as a helicopter crewchief/mechanic has taught me many things. Computer science and aviation may seem far apart but I believe that experience and knowledge can be applied anywhere—especially in computer science!

I like creating solutions to a problem with a given set of limitations. One may ask, what if there are no given limitations? The capacity of my knowledge is a limitation. Luckily, I like learning about different things. A new or old concept can be applied to another concept.

Personal Interest

I like modifying or dismantling mechanisms. As a child, I was always curious about how remote controlled toys worked. Small DC motors, miniature ligt bulbs, wires, and batteries were my favorite toys. It was interesting what kind of contraptions I could come up with what I had available.

Currently, I have a 1971 Datsun 240z that I enjoy working on. I use my creativity and military experience as a mechanic to maintain and modify my project car.
